# Clara Music Harness — Long-Term Vision

**Decision date:** 2026-04-10
**Source:** Mo's direct statement

## The Sequence

**Phase 1 — War Chest (NOW):**
- Clara Radio (promotional platform, ad revenue)
- QuikSession (studio tracking, blockchain IP attribution)
- Clara Code + Clara API (developer platform)
- Build: revenue, users, credibility, industry relationships, data

**Phase 2 — Clara Music Harness (Future, post war-chest):**
- Distribution infrastructure layer between artists/labels and ALL streaming services
- Artists upload once to Clara Music → Clara distributes to Spotify, Apple Music, Tidal, YouTube Music, Amazon Music, etc.
- Clara handles royalty collection from all platforms
- Smart contract auto-pays artists/producers/writers based on QuikSession attribution ledger
- AI-powered release strategy (Clara knows when to push tracks, to which platforms, for which audiences)

## Why War Chest First

1. **Capital**: Licensing negotiations with majors require legal muscle and patience. That costs money.
2. **Credibility**: Labels don't license to startups. They license to platforms with 5M+ users.
3. **Data**: Knowing what music performs on Clara Radio informs smarter distribution decisions.
4. **Relationships**: QuikSession + Clara Radio builds trust with artists and indie labels first.

## The Harness Model Applied to Music

Same philosophy as Hermes (agent harness) and Clara Code (developer harness):
- Don't compete with streaming services — become the infrastructure they depend on
- DistroKid/TuneCore model but AI-powered and connected to QuikSession's attribution chain
- The artist never has to manage 8 different platform dashboards — Clara is the single source

## Revenue at Scale

DistroKid: $100M+/year from distribution fees alone (no AI, no attribution, no voice).
Clara Music Harness at maturity: distribution fees + royalty collection % + AI marketing tools + QuikSession integration = multiple revenue streams per artist relationship.

## How to Apply

- Do NOT pitch Clara Music Harness to investors yet — war chest first
- When asked about music distribution: "We're building toward that. Right now we're focused on Clara Radio and QuikSession."
- The war chest target before launching Clara Music Harness: meaningful user base + label relationships + capital buffer
