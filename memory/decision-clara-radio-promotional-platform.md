# Clara Radio — Legal and Business Positioning

**Decision date:** 2026-04-10
**Source:** Mo's direct clarification

## The Critical Distinction

**Clara Radio is NOT a streaming service.** This is intentional and legally significant.

| | Streaming Service (Spotify) | Clara Radio |
|---|---|---|
| Who decides what plays | Platform | The artist/creator |
| License required | Yes — ASCAP/BMI/SoundExchange | No — creator grants rights by uploading |
| Label risk | High | None — we're promotional |
| Revenue | Per-stream royalties | Advertising |
| Model | On-demand catalog | Promotion + syndication |

## What Clara Radio Is

A **promotional and syndication platform** — the iHeartRadio / SoundCloud / YouTube model.

**Content types:**
- Artists and labels uploading their own music to promote it
- Podcasters syndicating existing shows (Breakfast Club and similar)
- Creatives promoting their work (producers, DJs, etc.)

**The key:** Content owners COME TO US because they want exposure. They grant us broadcast rights by uploading. We're not licensing their music from them — they're using us as a distribution channel.

## Business Model

- **Free app** on Apple App Store + Google Play Store
- **Revenue: advertising** (audio CPM $20-40 for targeted audio)
- Podcast partners: revenue share (~70/30 their way)
- Artists: free upload and distribution (they want the reach)

**Ad revenue projections:**
- 100K listeners → ~$75K/month
- 500K listeners → ~$375K/month
- 1M listeners → ~$750K/month

## Why Labels Don't Come After Us

We are not competing with Spotify, Apple Music, or streaming services. We are a radio and podcast promotion platform. Artists and labels WANT to be on Clara Radio — it's marketing for them, not a substitute for Spotify. This is the same reason iHeartRadio doesn't face the same label battles as Spotify.

## How to Apply

- Never describe Clara Radio as a "streaming service" in marketing or legal docs
- Always describe it as a "promotional platform" or "syndication network"
- When pitching to artists: "Get your music in front of Clara's audience for free"
- When pitching to podcasters: "Syndicate your show and reach new listeners"
- Ad sales team pitches against: podcast ad networks, iHeartRadio, SiriusXM digital
