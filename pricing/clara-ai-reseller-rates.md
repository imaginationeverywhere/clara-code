# Clara AI Reseller Rates

Wholesale pricing for partners who resell Clara AI consumer tiers. **Reseller margins are designed to start HIGH (50%+) at the Starter tier and collapse to 10% at Elite** — because the model is **volume, not per-seat markup**. A reseller with 100,000 seats at 10% margin makes orders of magnitude more than a reseller with 100 seats at 50% margin.

**This file is for Clara AI ONLY (consumer tiers $9-$59).** Clara Code builder tiers do not have a reseller program.

---

## The Goal: 10,000,000 Agents Through Resellers

Clara's growth target is 10M seats through reseller channels + 10M direct seats = 20M total. Reseller pricing is engineered for that scale:

- **Thin per-seat margins** force resellers to scale.
- **Aggressive volume discounts** reward resellers who deliver volume.
- **Locked maximum discount (25% off wholesale at 1M+ seats)** protects Clara's margin even at full scale.

Resellers aren't selling boutique — they're doing volume distribution. A 100-seat reseller is a side hustle. A 100,000-seat reseller is a real business. A 1M-seat reseller is a strategic partner.

## Track 1 — White Label Reseller (their brand, Clara invisible)

**Customer sees:** [Reseller brand] only
**Reseller sets their own retail price** (minimum = wholesale)

| Tier | Retail (our ref) | Wholesale | Reseller Margin @ Retail |
|------|-------------------|-----------|--------------------------|
| Starter $9 | $9 | **$5** | 44% |
| Lite $19 | $19 | **$13** | 32% |
| Standard $29 | $29 | **$22** | 24% |
| Plus $39 | $39 | **$32** | 18% |
| Pro $49 | $49 | **$42** | 14% |
| Elite $59 | $59 | **$53** | **10%** |

Margin shape: **44% → 10%** as tiers climb. Reseller leans into volume at Pro/Elite; takes fat margins at Starter/Lite to attract their market.

## Track 2 — Clara Branded Reseller (sells AS Clara)

Slightly better discounts than White Label because Clara gets brand exposure. Customer sees Clara's logo.

**Customer sees:** "Clara AI, sold by [Reseller]"
**Reseller MUST undercut Clara retail** (or sell at retail max, not above)

| Pro $49 | $49 | **$35** | $45 | 22% |
| Elite $59 | $59 | **$44** | $55 | 20% |

Margin shape: **53% → 20%** as tiers climb.

## Volume Discounts — The Real Profit Lever

Applied on top of wholesale. Monthly evaluation based on active seats.

| Active Seats | Discount Off Wholesale |
|--------------|------------------------|
| 1-99 | 0% (base) |
| 100-999 | 5% |
| 1,000-9,999 | 10% |
| 10,000-99,999 | 15% |
| 100,000-999,999 | 20% |
| **1,000,000+** | **25%** (Tier-1 Strategic Partner, custom contract) |

The goal is 10M agents through resellers — so the top discount tier at 1M+ is the one that matters most economically. Resellers at that scale become strategic partners (custom contracts, dedicated account management, revenue share on overage and marketplace beyond what's in this table).

### How Volume Economics Unlock Reseller Profitability

**White Label reseller at Standard $29 tier:**

| Scale | Effective Wholesale | Reseller Margin at $29 retail | Monthly Profit (at scale) |
|-------|--------------------|--------------------------------|----------------------------|
| 100 seats (5% off $22) | $20.90 | 28% | $810 |
| 1,000 seats (10% off $22) | $19.80 | 32% | $9,200 |
| 10,000 seats (15% off $22) | $18.70 | 36% | $103,000 |
| 100,000 seats (20% off $22) | $17.60 | 39% | $1,140,000 |
| 1,000,000 seats (25% off $22) | $16.50 | 43% | $12,500,000 |

**At 1M seats, the reseller makes $12.5M/month at the $29 tier.** Even at 10% base margin on Elite, they'd make ~$5M/month. That's why thin per-seat margins + massive volume is the game.

## Overage Revenue Handling

### White Label

Reseller charges whatever they want for overage to their customers. Reseller pays Clara **$0.05/hr** flat wholesale overage (covers our $0.036/hr COGS + 39% margin). Reseller keeps the spread.

Example — reseller charges their customer $0.50/hr overage (mirroring our retail), customer runs 120 overage hours:
- Customer pays reseller: $60
- Reseller pays Clara: $6 ($0.05 × 120)
- Reseller profit on overage: $54

### Clara Branded

Overage is collected by Clara at retail rates ($0.50/hr on Standard, $0.25/hr on Plus, etc.). **Reseller gets 50% of Clara's overage revenue** as their partner cut. Clara handles the billing; reseller sees it on their payout report.

Example — Clara Branded customer on $29 Standard goes 120 hrs over:
- Customer pays Clara: $60 (at $0.50/hr retail)
- Clara COGS: $4.32 ($0.036 × 120)
- Clara keeps: 50% of $60 = $30; Clara margin: $30 - $4.32 = $25.68
- Reseller gets: 50% of $60 = $30

## Marketplace Revenue Share (Reseller's Customer Hires a Talent Agency Agent)

**White Label:** No Talent Agency access by default. Reseller's users are locked to their branded ecosystem. (Reseller can opt in with standard 85/15 terms.)

**Clara Branded:** Full Talent Agency access. When reseller's customer hires an agent (e.g., Pookey's barber bot):
- Creator: 85%
- Clara: 15%
- Reseller gets **20% of Clara's 15%** (3% of gross) as distribution royalty

---

## White Label Qualification (Gate for Access)

White label is premium. To qualify, reseller must meet ONE of:

- $5,000+ upfront OR signed annual contract
- 500,000+ authentic social media following
- Established brand / agency with existing book of business
- 100+ seats/month minimum commitment

Clara Branded has no qualification beyond identity verification — anyone can become a Clara Branded reseller.

## Illustrative Examples

### Example 1 — Fitness Influencer, White Label at Standard Tier, 10K Seats

- Sells branded "FitCoachAI" at $29/mo to 10,000 followers
- Wholesale: $22/seat → 15% volume discount at 10K = $18.70/seat
- Revenue: $29 × 10,000 = $290,000/mo
- Cost to reseller: $18.70 × 10,000 = $187,000/mo
- **Reseller net: $103,000/mo (36% margin)**
- Clara revenue: $187,000/mo; COGS ~$52,000 (typical use); **Clara net ~$135,000/mo (72% margin)**

### Example 2 — Corporate Partner, White Label at Elite, 100K Seats

- Enterprise employee benefit program — 100,000 employees with Clara AI Elite
- Wholesale: $53/seat → 20% volume discount at 100K = $42.40/seat
- Revenue (they charge their members via benefits dept): $59 × 100,000 = $5,900,000/mo
- Cost to reseller: $42.40 × 100,000 = $4,240,000/mo
- **Reseller net: $1,660,000/mo (28% margin)**
- Clara net: ~$3,200,000/mo (75% margin at typical 40% usage)

### Example 3 — Strategic Partner, 1M Seats Across Tiers

A major streaming platform bundles Clara AI with their premium tier. 1M seats — 70% Starter, 20% Standard, 10% Pro.
- 700K × $5 (Starter wholesale × 25% off = $3.75) = $2.625M/mo
- 200K × $22 (Standard × 25% off = $16.50) = $3.3M/mo
- 100K × $42 (Pro × 25% off = $31.50) = $3.15M/mo
- **Total Clara revenue: $9.075M/mo**
- Clara COGS at typical use: ~$1.5M
- **Clara net: ~$7.5M/mo (83% margin)**

At 10M seats across resellers (the target), Clara revenue ≈ $75-100M/mo, net ~$60-80M/mo.

## Why This Pricing Supports the 10M-Seat Vision

| Lever | Mechanism | Outcome at 10M seats |
|-------|-----------|----------------------|
| Thin per-seat wholesale margin for resellers | Forces volume | Only serious resellers scale |
| Aggressive volume discount (0-25%) | Rewards scale | Partners bring seats to earn discount |
| Fat margin for Clara at high tiers | Protects us at scale | $60-80M/mo net at 10M seats |
| Hard wholesale floor (no custom below 25%) | Sustainable unit economics | We never race to the bottom |
| Clara Branded > White Label margins | Incentivizes brand exposure | More Clara Branded partners = better brand awareness |
