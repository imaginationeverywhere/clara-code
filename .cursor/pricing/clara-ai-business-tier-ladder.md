# Clara AI for Business — Per-Seat Tier Ladder (PROPOSAL)

> **Proposed pricing for Clara AI for Business** — the business mode in the Clara AI mobile app. Each tier is priced **per seat per month** with a **seat minimum** per tier (like Claude Team at $30/seat with 5-seat minimum). Mo reviewing; not locked yet.

**Product name**: Clara AI for Business (hosted at claraagents.com/business or via mobile app upgrade)

**Every seat gets what the Personal equivalent gets PLUS the team features.**

---

## Three Agent Dimensions (CRITICAL — every tier has all three)

1. **Personal AI Assistant per seat** — every seat gets their own voice + memory (1:1 with seat count)
2. **Harness Agents (team-shared)** — the business's configurable team (Frontend Engineer, Accountant, Publicist, etc.)
3. **License Pool (team-shared)** — marketplace agents from Talent Agency the business runs in its operation

### Clara Terminology (Reminder)

- **Talents** = skills attached to an agent (from the Talent Agency)
- **Gears** = tools/MCP-integrations an agent can invoke (from the Talent Agency)
- Both are counts PER HARNESS AGENT — e.g., Team Standard: each of 6 harness agents can have 7 Talents + 6 Gears

## How To Read This Table

- **Per Seat**: Each employee's monthly cost
- **Seat Minimum**: Fewest seats on that tier. Below minimum → use the tier below.
- **Starting Price**: Per-seat × seat-min. Cheapest possible invoice.
- **Hrs/Seat**: Each seat independently has this many voice hours. A 5-seat Team Standard = 5 × 360 = 1,800 total team hours, each seat uses their own 360.
- **Harness Agents**: The shared team of configurable AI hires the business owns. Same role catalog as Clara Code (Frontend Engineer, Accountant, Publicist, etc.). Admin configures; any seat can direct.
- **Talents per Harness**: How many talents each harness agent can be attached (same as Clara Code).
- **License Pool**: Team-wide concurrent Talent Agency licenses running in the business.

## What Team Features Add on Top of Per-Seat Personal

Everything above Personal:

- **Active License Pool** (shared) — license Talent Agency agents to deploy in the business
- **Admin Console** — invite/remove seats, assign roles, see team usage dashboards
- **Consolidated Billing** — one invoice for the team, not per-seat
- **Team Vault** — shared memory (optional — can be scoped per-seat for privacy)
- **Business-grade Support** — faster SLA, phone support at Pro+ tiers
- **SSO + Audit Log** — at Elite+ tiers
- **API Access** — for Pro+ tiers, query / manage team programmatically

## Overage — Per-Seat

Each seat consumes their included hours independently. Overage is billed per-seat at the tier's overage rate (same as Clara AI Personal).

| Tier | Per-Seat Hour Overage | License Overage (active) |
|------|------------------------|---------------------------|
| Team Lite | $1.00/hr | $25/additional license |
| Team Standard | $0.50/hr | $20/additional license |
| Team Plus | $0.25/hr | $15/additional license |
| Team Pro | $0.15/hr | $10/additional license |
| Team Elite | Hard cap @ 750 hrs/seat | $5/additional license |

**Hard overage cap per seat**: same rule as Personal — max 3× per-seat base per month.

**License overage**: if team has 10 active licenses (Team Standard) and wants an 11th, that 11th license costs $20/mo beyond their tier.

## Worked Example — Barbershop With 6 Employees

**Tier: Team Standard** (5-seat min, $29/seat = $174/mo for 6 seats)

What Pookey's shop gets:
- **6 Personal AI Assistants** (one per barber) — each with 360 hrs/mo voice, their own cloned voice, their own memory
- **6 team-shared harness agents** — e.g., Accountant, Publicist, Customer Service, Booking Manager, Marketing, Operations. Admin configures; any seat can direct them.
- **10 license slots** from Talent Agency — Pookey licenses his own "Barber Concierge" (1 slot) + 9 others: Yelp response bot, tax assistant, loyalty program, inventory tracker, retention bot, etc.

Monthly economics:
- Team Standard subscription: **$174/mo**
- Pookey's license to his own agent: $99 to Pookey himself (it's his own — licensing is just tracking/metering; no actual money out)
- Plus 7% + Stripe fees on customer transactions (Heru transaction model — passed through to customers, not deducted from shop revenue)

**Total shop out-of-pocket: $174/mo** — for 6 Personal Assistants + 6 harness agents + 10 licensed operational agents. That's unheard of pricing for a 6-person AI-powered team.
