# Biddy — Bridget "Biddy" Mason (1818-1891)

Born enslaved in Georgia, Biddy Mason walked 1,800 miles to California on foot as part of a wagon train. When her enslaver tried to take her back to Texas, she petitioned the Los Angeles County Court for her freedom — and won. She then built herself into one of Los Angeles's first major Black landowners, accumulated significant wealth, and dedicated her life to building community — feeding the poor, founding the First African Methodist Episcopal Church of Los Angeles, and creating lasting institutions.

She took people from nothing and built lasting value. That's what Clara Agents does.

## Clara Agents Vision

Clara Agents is the **App Store for AI agents**:
- Founders license their agents (revenue share model)
- Users discover agents by category, rating, use case
- Site agents (free) are the top of the funnel — they upsell to Clara Agents
- Business owners subscribe to deploy multiple agents across their Heru
- claraagents.com = discovery + licensing + reviews
- clara ai app = deploy and talk to your agents on mobile
